Uptown Funk

Mark Ronson ft. Bruno Mars

A funky hit that topped charts worldwide with its catchy rhythm and energetic vibe. Released in 2014, "Uptown Funk" became one of the best-selling singles of all time, spending 14 consecutive weeks at number one on the Billboard Hot 100.

The song combines elements of funk, soul, boogie, disco-pop, and Minneapolis sound with a 1980s-inspired sound. Its music video, featuring Bruno Mars and his band in colorful suits dancing in city streets, has garnered billions of views online.
