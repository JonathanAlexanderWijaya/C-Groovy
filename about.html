About C'Groovy

Learn about our journey, our mission, and the team behind C'Groovy.

Our Journey

2016

C'Groovy was founded by a group of music enthusiasts and tech innovators with a vision to revolutionize how people discover and enjoy music.

2017

Launched our beta platform with a library of 10,000 songs and gained our first 50,000 users through word-of-mouth marketing.

2018

Secured Series A funding and expanded our library to include over 1 million songs from artists around the world.

2019

Introduced our personalized recommendation algorithm and reached 1 million active monthly users.

2020

Launched our mobile app for iOS and Android, making C'Groovy accessible on-the-go for music lovers everywhere.

2022

Expanded into podcasts and exclusive artist content, creating a more diverse audio experience for our users.

2024

Celebrating over 10 million active users and continuing to innovate with new features and partnerships in the music industry.

Our Mission

To bring music to everyone's fingertips, creating a platform where users can discover, stream, and share songs that resonate with them, anytime and anywhere.

Our Vision

To be the world's leading music streaming service, leveraging technology to provide a seamless listening experience, personalized recommendations, and innovative features that create an interactive and dynamic music community.
